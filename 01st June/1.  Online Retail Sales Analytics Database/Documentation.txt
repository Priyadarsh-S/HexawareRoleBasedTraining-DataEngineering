Given Scenario:
A retail company sells products online across multiple Indian cities. The company wants to analyze:
    Customers
    Products
    Orders
    Payments
    Delivery status
    Revenue
    Customer behavior
The goal is to build a MySQL database and write analytical SQL queries for management reporting.

Online Retail Sales Analytics Database
Database Design:
    This Database is designed for an online retail company that sells products across different cities in India. The database contains six tables: customers, products, orders, order_items, payments, and deliveries. These tables help store and manage information related to customers, products, sales transactions, payments, and delivery status.

Table Relationships:
    The customers table is connected to the orders table through customer_id.
    The orders table is connected to the order_items table through order_id.
    The products table is linked to the order_Items table through product_id.
    The payments table and deliveries table are connected to the orders table through order_id.

Key Insights from Reports:
    Hyderabad has the highest number of customers in the database.
    Electronics products generate higher revenue compared to other categories.
    Some customers have placed multiple orders, showing repeat purchases.
    UPI is one of the most commonly used payment methods.
    A few orders have failed payments and pending deliveries, which can help identify operational issues.
